You have landed on the website of Walter Sarocouze, a writer of transgressive fiction.

This is not entertainment. If you are easily disturbed, close the page now and do not return.

The texts gathered here were, until recently, confined to private circulation — printed in small runs, handed quietly from one reader to another. What you will find is not curated for comfort. It does not seek your approval.

In time, other voices may join these pages. Voices that, like Walter's, refuse to look away.

You are still here? Then understand: this is a space where literature is under no obligation to be polite.

The site is maintained by Nick Zavadsky — Walter's student, friend, and assistant.
